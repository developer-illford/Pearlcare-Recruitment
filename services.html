Senior HealthCare Assistant

As a Senior Health Care Assistant, you will play a key role in providing essential care and support within a healthcare setting. Your responsibilities include assisting with daily activities such as personal hygiene, mobility, and vital sign monitoring. In addition to direct patient care, you will supervise and provide guidance to a team of healthcare assistants, ensuring adherence to protocols and high-quality standards. Effective communication with patients, families, and the healthcare team is crucial, and you will maintain accurate patient records.

Chefs/Catering Assistants

Our Chefs and Catering Assistants are dedicated to providing nutritious and satisfying meals that support the well-being and recovery of those in our care. They carefully prepare a wide range of meals, tailored to meet specific dietary needs and preferences, ensuring that each dish contributes to the health and comfort of both patients and staff. In addition to preparing food, our chefs uphold the highest standards of hygiene and kitchen safety, strictly following all health and safety protocols. They also play an important role in planning and organizing meal schedules, working closely with dietitians to understand the unique needs of each individual. With effective communication and meticulous attention to detail, our Chefs and Catering Assistants help create a compassionate and supportive environment, making meal times a moment of care and nourishment for everyone involved.
